Operational Blueprint — Score Reduction Guide
Guidelines to Lower Your Score
These are not suggestions. They are architectural requirements for Non-Resistant Scale.
↓ Reduce Pipeline Gaps
1. Install a Qualification GateEvery lead must pass a 3-question digital filter before accessing the Principal's time.

2. Codify the Proposal ProtocolA written, non-negotiable process from first contact to signed contract — followed identically every time.

3. Deploy a CRM DashboardEvery lead visible in one place. No lead lives in an inbox or in someone's memory.

4. Build a Repeatable Outreach MachineTarget: 60% system-generated revenue, 40% referral.
↓ Reduce Decision Lag
1. Create a Decision Authority MatrixDefine which decisions require the Principal, which are delegated, and at what threshold.

2. The 48-Hour Decision ProtocolEvery strategic decision has a 48-hour deadline. No commitment by day four = escalate to a decision.

3. Distribute Data, Not InstructionsGive your team the information required to decide without escalating. KPI dashboards are mandatory.

4. Pre-approve Pricing ExceptionsDefine a pricing band within which the team operates without approval.
↓ Reduce System Misalignment
1. Implement an ERP / Odoo SystemYour tools must enforce your strategy — not store it. An ERP tracking delivery promises against capacity is non-negotiable.

2. Define the Delivery Promise ProtocolCreate a scope matrix. No promises beyond capacity. No exceptions.

3. Weekly Calendar AuditEvery task the Principal performs below their leverage tier is a systems failure. Identify and delegate.

4. Build the 14-Day Offline ProtocolDocument every action taken in 2 weeks. This becomes the delegation manual.
