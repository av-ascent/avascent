The Proprietary Framework
FRICTION IS NOT A FEELING.
IT IS A CALCULATION.
Three variables. One precise number. The F-Score tells you exactly how much operational chaos is costing your firm — and which mandate will end it.
F =  [ (PG × 5) + (DL × 3) + (SM × 2) ] × C
C = Complexity Multiplier: ×1.0 small  ·  ×1.5 mid-size  ·  ×2.0 large or regulated
PG
Pipeline Gaps
Weight ×5  ·  Highest Impact
Where is qualified revenue leaking? Untracked leads, no standard proposal process, non-systemic BD that dies when you're unavailable. Scored 1–10 during the session. Every point here is direct lost top-line revenue.

DL
Decision Lag
Weight ×3  ·  Most Personal
How fast do decisions move from question to action? The consensus trap, authority debt, and the number of decisions that currently require your personal sign-off. The most personal variable — and the most honest mirror of your leadership structure.
Phase 2 · 25 minutes of the diagnostic
SM
System Misalignment
Weight ×2  ·  Silent Tax
The disconnect between your vision and your infrastructure. The 14-day test: if you went completely offline for 14 days — no calls, no approvals — what actually keeps moving? System misalignment is the variable that compounds invisibly over years.
